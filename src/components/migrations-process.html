01. The Migration Protocol

01

Full Site Audit

We use tools like Screaming Frog to crawl your existing URLs, assets, and metadata to ensure nothing is left behind.

02

301 Redirect Map

We export your current redirects and import them into HubSpot to maintain SEO equity and prevent 404 errors.

03

CMS Reconstruction

We don't just copy; we recreate layouts using HubSpot's drag-and-drop themes or custom modules for easier future editing.

04

DNS Switch

We pre-provision SSL certificates and handle the DNS switch to ensure minimal downtime during the live launch.
