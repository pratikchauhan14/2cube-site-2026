[SEO]

Will my SEO rankings drop during migration?

No. We implement a strict 301 Redirect Strategy. We map every single old URL to its new location, telling Google that the page has permanently moved, preserving 100% of your link equity.

[UPTIME]

Will my site go down during the process?

Zero downtime. We perform the entire migration on a HubSpot Sandbox environment. Your live site stays active until the final DNS switch, which happens instantly.

[EDITING]

Can I edit the website myself after launch?

Absolutely. We build custom Drag-and-Drop Modules. You can change text, images, and layout structure without touching a single line of code.

[DATA]

Do you migrate blog comments?

Yes, for WordPress we can export comments. However, many clients switch to Disqus for better spam control. We will discuss this during the audit phase.
